Topics with Theory

1. Algebra

Algebra deals with variables and equations. It includes solving linear equations, quadratic equations, and simplifying expressions. Example: Solve 2x + 3 = 7.

2. Trigonometry

Trigonometry studies relationships between angles and sides of triangles. Important identities: sin²θ + cos²θ = 1

3. Calculus

Calculus is the study of change. Derivatives measure rate of change. Example: Derivative of x² = 2x.
